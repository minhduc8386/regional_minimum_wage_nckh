## Source Groups

### Double Machine Learning

- Chernozhukov et al. (2018), "Double/debiased machine learning for treatment and structural parameters."
- Key use: justify DML only after checking that flexible nuisance models add value.
- Source: https://academic.oup.com/ectj/article/21/1/C1/5056401

### International Minimum Wage and Employment

- Card and Krueger (1994), New Jersey/Pennsylvania fast food, DiD/case study.
- Dube, Lester, and Reich (2010), contiguous county border design.
- Cengiz, Dube, Lindner, and Zipperer (2019), low-wage jobs and prominent US minimum wage changes.
- Neumark and Wascher (2008), broader review/book with more skeptical employment interpretation.

### Developing Countries and Informality

- Comola and de Mello (2009), Indonesia, decentralised minimum-wage setting, formal/informal/unemployment outcomes.
- Lemos (2004/2009), Brazil, formal and informal sectors.
- Rama (2001), Indonesia, large minimum wage increase.
- Bell (1997), Mexico and Colombia; needs full-text verification.
- Maloney and Nuñez (2004), Latin America; needs full-text verification.

### Vietnam

- Nguyen Viet Cuong (2013), VHLSS 2004/2006, DiD + PSM, low-wage workers and formal-sector job loss/self-employment.
- Del Carpio, Nguyen, Nguyen, and Wang (2013), Vietnam wages, employment, welfare, self-employment.
- Phan Kim Dung (2017), Vietnamese micro firms and SMEs, regional wage variation.
- Nguyen Viet Cuong (2025), LFS 2012-2020, district and province-year fixed effects, employment/hours/earnings.
- ILO (2024/2026), recent Vietnam minimum wage policy reports using LFS, enterprise census, and qualitative material.
